FAQ

Common questions.

What platforms does the engine post to? +
14 platforms: Telegram, Discord, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky, LinkedIn, Threads, Pinterest, Medium, Reddit, Quora via Buffer and direct API. TikTok, YouTube and Twitter/X are being onboarded.
Does it run fully automatically? +
Yes. Once the Google Apps Script trigger is set, the engine runs on your schedule with zero manual input. It generates content, creates images, posts to all platforms, marks rows complete, and resets the cycle automatically.
How does the same product get posted without being repetitive? +
Every cycle the AI generates a completely fresh angle — different hook, different psychological stage, different emotional driver. The variation matrix ensures no two runs produce the same output even for the same product.
Are affiliate links always included? +
In SELL and CTA stage posts, yes — always. But they link to the website first, never naked affiliate links. In ATTRACT, ENGAGE and TRUST posts, no link appears intentionally. Those posts build trust first.
What AI models power the content? +
GROQ LLaMA-3.3-70B is primary — fast, high quality, niche-aware. Gemini 2.5 Flash and Mistral are automatic fallbacks. All three receive identical platform-tailored prompts so quality never drops on fallback.
